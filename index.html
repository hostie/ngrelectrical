About Us

NGR Electrical Ltd is an electrical installation and maintenance company that prides itself on excellent electrical services to your home or place of business.

We are registered with NAPIT, which means our qualifications, insurance, work history and references have all been thoroughly checked using NAPIT's vetting process. All of our jobs, no matter how small, always come with a form of certification for added peace of mind.

Formed by our Director, Ashley Curtis in 2013. Our experience spans over 6 years in the industry. Being a time served electrician you can rest assured that you will receive high quality workmanship whenever you use our services.

Although a newly established company, NGR Electrical Ltd has been able to provide first class electrical installation and maintenance services to customers ranging from; schools, shops, landlords, offices, restaurants and home owners. We strive on perfection and we are extremely customer focused.
